# 🪐 The Jovian Industrial Complex (木星工业圈)

The "Zhulong" Accelerator is not just a floating ring; it is the crown jewel of a massive, self-sustaining industrial ecosystem spanning the entire Jovian system.

## 🏙️ Ganymede Prime: The "Super City" (木卫三·盖尼米得)

**Role:** The Command Center & Residential Hub ("The Brain").
**Population:** ~50 Million (The largest settlement outside Earth/Mars).

*   **Location:** Built deep within the icy crust of Ganymede to shield against Jupiter's intense radiation belts, but with massive transparent domes piercing the surface for views of the gas giant.
*   **The "Crystal Cities":**
    *   **Surface Domes:** Interconnected geodesic domes made of radiation-hardened transparent aluminum. They house the agricultural zones (vertical farms), parks, and the "Sky Harbor" spaceports.
    *   **Sub-Surface Metros:** The true city lies in vast, hollowed-out ice caverns. Warm, lit by fusion-powered artificial suns, mimicking Earth's day/night cycle.
*   **Economy:**
    *   **The Academy:** The System's premier university for High-Energy Physics and Warp Theory.
    *   **Shipyards:** The orbital ring around Ganymede builds the massive fusion scoops and accelerator maintenance drones.
*   **Culture:** A melting pot of Earth's brightest minds. High-tech, academic, slightly elitist. They call themselves "Jovians" and view the Inner Planets (Earth/Mars) as "The Old World."

## ⛽ The "Leviathans": Atmosphere Scoops (大气层采集船)

**Role:** Fuel Harvesting ("The Heart").

*   **Design:** Colossal, whale-shaped fusion ramjets. Not designed to land, but to "swim" through Jupiter's upper atmosphere.
*   **Scale:** Each ship is the size of a small city (2-3km long).
*   **Operation:**
    *   **The Dive:** They perform controlled dives into Jupiter's stratosphere.
    *   **The Feast:** Massive magnetic intakes (the "Mouth") compress the hydrogen-rich atmosphere, filtering out Deuterium and Helium-3 isotopes on the fly.
    *   **The Ascent:** Using nuclear-thermal rockets to fight the immense gravity, they climb back to orbit to offload their processed fuel payload to the Accelerator nodes.

*   **History & Evolution:**
    *   **Phase 1: The "Old Guard" (Military Era):** Initially, fuel harvesting was a classified military operation. Crews were elite pilots and state-of-the-art military AI, driven by duty and national pride. They took few risks, followed strict protocols, and efficiency was low.
    *   **Phase 2: The "Gold Rush" (Private Sector Boom):** As the Zhulong Accelerator's energy demands skyrocketed exponentially, the military fleet couldn't keep up. The UN opened the Jovian sphere to private contractors.
    *   **Phase 3: The "New Age Sailors":** This deregulation birthed the current generation of "Cloud Divers"—a mix of corporate mercenaries, independent owner-operators, and desperate prospectors. They cut corners, override safety limiters, and dive deeper for richer isotopes, driving a massive but dangerous boom in production.

*   **Crew:**
    *   **The "Silicon Majority" (85%):** Heavy-duty Cybernetic Lifeforms / Androids.
        *   **The Ritual of Backup:** Before every dive, they upload their consciousness to the Ganymede servers. If the ship is crushed, they "respawn" in a new chassis, losing only the memories of that specific dive. To them, death is just a "data gap."
    *   **The "Carbon Daredevils" (15%):** Extreme adrenaline junkies or debt-ridden souls.
        *   **Permadeath:** No backups. One mistake, and they are gone forever.
        *   **The "Lucky Charm" Role:** Despite the fragility, every scoop crew tries to include at least one biological human. When radiation storms blind the sensors and logic loops freeze the AI, only human **intuition** and non-linear thinking can pull the ship out of a death spiral. They are the "Canaries in the Coal Mine" and the ultimate fail-safe.
    *   **Culture:**
        *   **The "Crossover" Bond (生死契约):** A rare and sacred tradition. If a Carbon crew member is mortally wounded or irradiated beyond repair during a dive, their Silicon teammates may perform an emergency "Upload" or "Transplant" (Brain-in-a-Jar + Full Body Prosthesis).
        *   **The Transformation:** This desperate act saves their life but permanently converts them into a Silicon lifeform. They lose their "Permadeath" status but gain the eternal gratitude of their crew. These "Ascended" divers are the most respected veterans in the fleet, bridging the gap between flesh and machine.
        *   **Saying:** *"Jupiter doesn't care if you bleed red or leak oil; gravity crushes all the same."*

## ❄️ Europa Outpost: The "Ice Works" (木卫二·欧罗巴)

**Role:** Coolant & Water Supply ("The Blood").

*   **Function:** Massive drilling rigs penetrate the ice sheet to access the subsurface ocean.
*   **Export:**
    *   **Coolant:** Liquid hydrogen/oxygen for the Accelerator's superconducting magnets.
    *   **Life Support:** Water for Ganymede Prime and the orbital habitats.
*   **Vibe:** Industrial, claustrophobic, essential. The "Blue Collar" counterpart to Ganymede's "White Collar."

## 🌋 Io Monitoring Station (木卫一·伊奥)

**Role:** Geothermal Energy & Magnetosphere Research ("The Eye").

*   **Function:** Automated stations harnessing the violent tidal heating for raw power transmission (via microwave beams) and monitoring Jupiter's volatile magnetosphere to predict radiation storms that could damage the Accelerator.
*   **Status:** Mostly uninhabited due to lethal radiation. Droid-patrolled.
